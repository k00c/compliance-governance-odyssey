# Certifications

This directory tracks professional certifications, course completions, and credentials earned throughout your learning journey.

## Recommended Certifications for WA Resource Sector Governance

### Foundation Level
- ISO 31000 Risk Management Foundation
- Introduction to Mining Compliance (industry-specific courses)
- Environmental Compliance Basics
- Occupational Health & Safety Awareness

### Intermediate Level
- Certificate IV in Work Health and Safety
- Certified Internal Auditor (CIA) - if pursuing audit focus
- Environmental Management Systems Auditor (ISO 14001)
- GRC Professional (GRCP) certification

### Advanced Level
- Certified Compliance & Ethics Professional (CCEP)
- Certificate IV in Government (Workplace Governance)
- Advanced Diploma of Business (Governance)

## Tracking Your Progress

Create a markdown file for each certification pursuit with:
- Certification name and issuing body
- Requirements and prerequisites
- Study timeline
- Exam dates (if applicable)
- Completion date and credential number
- Link to digital badge or certificate

## Guidelines

- Research WA-specific certifications valued by mining companies
- Prioritize industry-recognized credentials
- Consider employer preferences in your target companies
- Balance cost with career advancement value
- Track CPD (Continuing Professional Development) requirements
