# Week 07: Governance Frameworks and Ethics

## Overview

**Focus Area:** Corporate Governance, Ethics, and Organizational Frameworks  
**Duration:** 1 Week  
**Estimated Time Commitment:** 18-22 hours

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1. Understand corporate governance principles and frameworks
2. Explain the role of boards, committees, and management in governance
3. Apply ethical decision-making frameworks to compliance scenarios
4. Understand corporate social responsibility in mining sector
5. Learn about governance maturity models
6. Develop ethics policies and codes of conduct
7. Understand whistleblowing and speak-up culture
8. Apply governance principles to WA resource sector context

## Key Topics

### Day 1-2: Governance Fundamentals
- ASX Corporate Governance Principles (4th edition)
- Board roles and responsibilities
- Governance structures in mining companies
- Regulatory governance requirements in WA

### Day 3-4: Ethics and Decision-Making
- Ethical frameworks and theories
- Conflicts of interest management
- Gift and entertainment policies
- Ethical decision-making models
- Case studies in mining ethics

### Day 5-6: Practical Application
- Develop code of conduct for mining operation
- Create ethics policy framework
- Design whistleblower protection program
- Analyze governance case studies from WA mining sector

### Day 7: Review and Consolidation
- Complete weekly deliverables
- Reflect on governance principles
- Prepare for Week 08

## Key Resources

### Essential Reading
- ASX Corporate Governance Principles and Recommendations (4th Edition)
- AICD Good Governance Principles
- WA Public Sector Code of Ethics
- "Governance and the Board" - selected chapters

### Practical Projects
1. **Ethics Policy Development:** Create comprehensive ethics policy for hypothetical mining company
2. **Governance Framework Analysis:** Evaluate governance structure of WA mining company
3. **Ethical Scenario Resolution:** Work through 5 ethical dilemmas using decision frameworks
