# Weeks 11-12: Stakeholder Engagement & Communication (Milestone 5)

## Overview

**Focus Area:** Multi-Level Stakeholder Communication and Regulatory Liaison  
**Duration:** 2 Weeks  
**Estimated Time Commitment:** 18-22 hours per week (36-44 hours total)  
**Milestone Project:** Comprehensive Stakeholder Communication Package

## Learning Objectives

1. Master communication strategies for different audiences
2. Develop regulatory liaison and response capabilities
3. Create executive-level reporting materials
4. Design community engagement approaches
5. Build internal compliance communication programs
6. Complete **Milestone Project 5:** Full communication package

## Milestone 5: Stakeholder Communication Package

### Scenario
WA mining company received DMIRS inspection with improvement notices. Must respond to regulator, update board, inform community, and communicate internally - all with different messaging approaches.

### Week 11: External Stakeholder Communication

**Part 1: Regulatory Response Package** (6-8 hours)
- Formal response letter to DMIRS acknowledging findings
- Detailed action plan addressing each finding
- Evidence package and documentation checklist
- Timeline for compliance demonstration
- Liaison strategy for ongoing regulator relationship

**Part 2: Board Reporting Materials** (5-6 hours)
- Monthly board compliance report format
- Executive dashboard summary (one-page visual)
- Risk and issue escalation protocols
- Compliance metrics and trends
- Regulatory relationship status

### Week 12: Community and Internal Communication

**Part 3: Community Communication** (4-5 hours)
- Community liaison briefing materials (plain language)
- FAQ document addressing common concerns
- Environmental protection commitments
- Community feedback mechanisms
- Contact information and engagement schedule

**Part 4: Internal Communication** (4-5 hours)
- Employee awareness materials (compliance expectations)
- Supervisor briefing guide (inspection preparation)
- Compliance newsletter template
- Reporting procedures and contact points
- Training communication plan

**Part 5: Integration and Polish** (3-4 hours)
- Ensure consistent messaging across all materials
- Professional formatting and branding
- Review for regulatory and legal considerations
- Final portfolio presentation

### Deliverables (All in `artifacts/case-studies/`)
1. Regulatory Response Letter and Action Plan
2. Board Compliance Report Template
3. Community Briefing Materials and FAQ
4. Employee Communication Package
5. Supervisor Briefing Guide

### Portfolio Value
Demonstrates sophisticated stakeholder management - ability to tailor compliance communications for regulators, board members, community, and employees. Shows political awareness and communication skills critical for senior compliance roles.
