## Progression Summary

### Specialization Phase (Weeks 13-17)
Builds depth in key compliance domains - digital governance, environmental, safety, regulatory change, and technology. Each week includes substantial practical project creating portfolio artifacts.

### Advanced Phase (Weeks 18-20)
Develops strategic competencies - business continuity, compliance technology strategy, and enterprise risk management. Prepares for senior compliance roles requiring strategic thinking.

### Time Investment
- Specialization: 90-115 hours across 5 weeks
- Advanced: 56-68 hours across 3 weeks
- **Total Weeks 13-20:** 146-183 hours

### Portfolio Artifacts Created
- 8 major project deliverables (1 per week)
- 25+ supporting documents
- Demonstrates breadth and depth across compliance domains
- Shows progressive skill development
- Provides interview talking points for diverse compliance areas
