## Weeks 21-22: Capstone A - Compliance Management System Design (30-40 hours)

### Objective
Design a comprehensive compliance management system (CMS) from the ground up for a mid-size WA mining company with no existing formal compliance infrastructure.

### Scenario
GoldenSands Mining Pty Ltd:
- Medium-size gold mining operation in Eastern Goldfields
- 350 employees, single operating mine + processing plant
- Growing company, transitioning from small operator to professional corporate
- Recently secured major investor, now requiring robust governance
- Board mandate: Establish world-class compliance function within 12 months
- Budget: $800K for year 1 implementation

### Week 21: CMS Framework and Design

**Day 1-2: Strategic Foundation** (8-10 hours)
- Compliance vision, mission, and objectives
- Governance structure (board oversight, management accountability)
- Compliance risk assessment and materiality
- Stakeholder analysis and engagement strategy
- Success metrics and KPIs
- 3-year strategic roadmap

**Day 3-4: Compliance Framework** (8-10 hours)
- Policy and procedure hierarchy design
- Compliance obligation register (comprehensive)
- Risk and control matrix
- Compliance calendar (all obligations mapped)
- Roles, responsibilities, and accountability matrix (RACI)
- Organizational structure recommendations

**Day 5-7: Monitoring and Assurance** (8-10 hours)
- Compliance monitoring framework
- Testing and assurance programs
- Compliance dashboard design
- Reporting protocols (board, management, operational)
- Issue and breach management processes
- Continuous improvement mechanisms

### Week 22: Implementation Planning and Documentation

**Day 1-2: Technology and Tools** (6-8 hours)
- Technology requirements specification
- GRC system recommendations
- Integration with existing systems
- Digital transformation roadmap
- Budget and resource planning

**Day 3-5: Implementation Roadmap** (10-12 hours)
- Phased implementation plan (Phases 1-3 over 12 months)
- Quick wins (30-60-90 days)
- Change management strategy
- Training and capability building program
- Communication plan
- Risk mitigation for implementation

**Day 6-7: Final Documentation** (8-10 hours)
- Complete CMS Design Document (40-60 pages)
- Executive presentation deck (15-20 slides)
- Business case and ROI analysis
- Implementation budget detailed breakdown
- Success measures and evaluation framework

## Week 23: Capstone B - Regulatory Change Impact Assessment (25-35 hours)

### Objective
Conduct comprehensive impact assessment of major regulatory change and develop full implementation plan for mining operation.

### Scenario
WA Government announces significant amendments to Environmental Protection Act affecting mine closure and rehabilitation obligations:
- New progressive rehabilitation requirements
- Enhanced financial assurance calculations
- Stricter closure criteria and post-mining land use standards
- Transition period: 18 months to full compliance
- Industry-wide impact on all WA mining operations

Your company: Pilbara Iron Ore Miner (3 active sites, 2 in care and maintenance, 850 employees)

### Week 23 Breakdown

**Day 1-2: Legislative Analysis** (8-10 hours)
- Detailed review of amended legislation
- Comparison with current requirements (gap analysis)
- Interpretation of ambiguous provisions
- Consultation with regulatory body (hypothetical)
- Industry association guidance review
- Legal risk assessment

**Day 3-4: Business Impact Assessment** (10-12 hours)
- Operational impact across 3 active sites
- Financial impact (capital and operating costs)
- Timeline impact (project delays, permits)
- Resource requirements (people, systems, external support)
- Competitive implications
- Strategic options analysis

**Day 4-5: Implementation Planning** (8-10 hours)
- Gap closure action plan (detailed)
- Project plan with milestones
- Resource allocation and budgeting
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Change management approach
- Stakeholder communication strategy
- Training requirements analysis

**Day 6-7: Final Package** (6-8 hours)
- Complete Impact Assessment Report (25-35 pages)
- Executive briefing (2-3 pages)
- Board presentation (12-15 slides)
- Budget submission with justification
- Implementation Gantt chart
- Communication materials (internal and external)

## Week 24: Capstone C - Enterprise Compliance Program Review (35-45 hours)

### Objective
Conduct comprehensive evaluation of existing compliance program, assess maturity, identify improvement opportunities, and develop enhancement roadmap.

### Scenario
PeakOre Mining Corporation (established WA diversified miner):
- 1,200 employees across 4 operations (gold, iron ore, lithium)
- Mature company with existing compliance function (5 years)
- Recent regulatory breaches and increasing internal concern
- New CEO wants independent review of compliance effectiveness
- Board requesting assurance that compliance fit for purpose

Your role: Independent compliance consultant conducting comprehensive program review

### Week 24 Breakdown

**Day 1-2: Current State Assessment** (10-12 hours)
- Compliance program documentation review
- Organizational structure and resources analysis
- Policy and procedure evaluation
- Technology and systems assessment
- Interviews with key stakeholders (hypothetical)
- Document review and evidence evaluation
- Regulatory relationship assessment

**Day 3-4: Maturity Evaluation** (8-10 hours)
- Compliance maturity model application
- Benchmarking against industry best practice
- Gap analysis across 8-10 dimensions:
  - Governance and oversight
  - Policy and procedures
  - Risk assessment
  - Monitoring and testing
  - Training and culture
  - Reporting and escalation
  - Technology and data
  - Continuous improvement
- Strengths and weaknesses identification
- Root cause analysis of recent breaches

**Day 5-6: Recommendations and Roadmap** (10-12 hours)
- Prioritized improvement recommendations (15-20)
- Quick wins vs strategic improvements
- 24-month enhancement roadmap
- Resource requirements and budgeting
- Change management strategy
- Success metrics and KPIs
- Implementation governance

**Day 7: Final Presentation** (8-10 hours)
- Complete Program Review Report (45-60 pages)
- Executive Summary (3-4 pages)

- Implementation tracker template

- **Final Portfolio Consolidation** - organize all 24 weeks of artifacts
