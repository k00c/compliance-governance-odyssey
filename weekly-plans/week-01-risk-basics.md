# Week 01: Risk Management Basics

## Overview

**Focus Area:** Foundational Risk Management Concepts for WA Resource Sector  
**Duration:** 1 Week  
**Estimated Time Commitment:** 15-20 hours

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1. Define key risk management terminology and concepts
2. Explain the risk management process according to ISO 31000
3. Identify and categorize risks in resource sector projects
4. Conduct a basic risk assessment using standard methodologies
5. Create and maintain a risk register
6. Understand WA-specific regulatory requirements for risk management

## Practical Projects

### Project 1: Sample Risk Register

**Objective:** Create a comprehensive risk register for a hypothetical mining exploration project in WA

**Scenario:** You are a compliance officer for a junior mining company beginning exploration activities in the Pilbara region. The project involves:
- Drill program across 3 tenements
- 15 staff including contractors
- Remote camp accommodation
- Heritage-sensitive areas nearby
- Seasonal weather considerations

**Requirements:**
1. Identify minimum 10 risks across these categories:
   - Occupational Health & Safety
   - Environmental
   - Regulatory/Compliance
   - Operational
   - Financial
   - Community/Stakeholder

2. For each risk, document:
   - Risk ID and description
   - Category
   - Likelihood (1-5 scale)
   - Consequence (1-5 scale)
   - Risk rating (L x C)
   - Risk owner
   - Existing controls
   - Proposed mitigation actions
   - Review date

3. Create a risk matrix visualization
